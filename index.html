Breathing Pattern Dysfunction Quiz

Welcome! This short quiz will help you gain insight into your breathing habits and patterns.

By the end, you’ll receive personalised feedback and guidance, along with options to book a session or explore helpful resources to support your breathing and wellbeing.

This quiz is informational and educational only and is not a medical diagnosis.

1. During activity or exercise, how does your breathing feel?

2. Overall, how much do breathing issues affect your daily life?

3. In the past 4 weeks, how often have you felt shortness of breath at rest or during mild activity?

4. How often do you feel unable to take a satisfying breath?

5. Do you mostly breathe through your mouth?

6. When you breathe, which area moves more?

7. Does stress or emotion make your breathing symptoms worse?

8. Do your breathing symptoms affect your sleep?

9. Do you avoid certain activities because of your breathing?

10. Do you feel limited in exercise or daily tasks due to your breathing?

11. Body Oxygen Level Test (BOLT): How many seconds can you comfortably hold your breath after a normal exhale?
